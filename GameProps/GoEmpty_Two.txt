<about>
Sow in either direction in a single lap. Sow both stores.

Repeat turn if last seed is sown into the either store. The number of
seeds collected in stores does not matter (and are not shown on the
game board).

Goal: If you clear all of your own seeds you win.

Variants:

- <nolink>GoEmpty_Two::MultiLap - sow in multiple laps and use 4 start seeds per hole (or the starter
can win on the opening move).
