Counter-clockwise, multi-lap sow.

Cross capture seeds on opponent's side.

The game is over when a player doesn't have a move on their turn or
when one player collects more than half of the seeds. The last mover
takes any seeds left on the board.

Goal: collect more than half the seeds.
