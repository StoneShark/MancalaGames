<about>
Single lap sow, counter-clockwise.

Capture on 2s, 4s and 6s.  Multiple capture previous holes sown to 2,
4 or 6 as long as an unbroken chain.

The game ends when the current player does not have any seeds. Any
remaining seeds go the last player to move.

Goal: collect more than half of the seeds.

- <nolink>Bohnenspiel::Rounds: play in rounds to 100 points
